About the Founder

Dr. Poornima Dore

Founder & CEO, Dorian Scale

Dr. Poornima Dore is an economist and strategic advisor with two decades of experience at the intersection of leadership, systems, and inclusive growth. Her work bridges academic rigor with practical impact, helping institutions design transformative policies and programs.

Specializing in regional development, institutional reform, and leadership systems, Poornima has advised governments, foundations, and global organizations on strategies for sustainable, equitable change.

"Meaningful change requires both structure and sensitivity."

Why Dorian Scale

At Dorian Scale, strategy is not just analytical — it is also intuitive, human, and deeply contextual. Like a scale in music, meaningful change requires structure, harmony, and movement.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Instead, we listen deeply, understand complex systems, and design pathways that respect local context while aspiring to global standards.

Our approach combines rigorous analysis with human-centered design, ensuring strategies are both intellectually sound and implementable within real-world constraints.
